jamTunes

jamTunes is a music social media platform, that allows users to create a custom profile, upload content, rate content, search for content, and jam with friends.

Create a Profile

Creating a profile is easy, just register an account on jamTunes, and on your profile page you can edit your profile information and update your status.

Upload Content

In order to upload a tune to jamTunes, you need to register an account. When you're logged in you can select Upload from the dropdown menu.

Rate Content

Jams that are uploaded can be voted on by any logged in user by clicking the 'Jam It' or 'Can It' buttons. User profiles display a total score called 'Jam Power' based on the votes given to content uploaded by the user.

Search for Content

Searching is done through the search bar at the top. Users can search for both uploaded content, as well other users on the site.

Jam with Friends

Logged in users can friend another user by visiting their profile and clicking the Add Friend button. You can view comments and uploads of your friends by visiting the friends page.
